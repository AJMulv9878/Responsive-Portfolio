About Me

Andrew grew up in Florida where he attended Apopka High School, Seminole State College and then finished his Computer Science coursework at UCF. He has always had a tremendous love for technology and the inner machinations on the internet. He is fascinated by the infrastructure behind the magnificent creation.

Andrew also has a very creative personality and background, he has extensively participated in the arts throughout his life including theatre and music. He decided to mix his two loves in a web designer profession. He loves being able to design eye catching modern websites that also pulls the user in with interactive services. Web designers are modern day Da Vinci's and the internet is their Louvre.

Besides his professional life, Andrew is also married to his beautiful wife Jennifer. They live together with their Shiba Inu "Ares", they plan on having children in the near future and continuing on creating their family.
